Doritos


George grabs his stack of Doritos from his kitchen and decides to pour all of it by his garage near the mousetraps. What will he do next?

He decides to wait and see if the mice will smell the Doritos and eat some for themselves. He thinks his little Doritos stack experiment with the mice will not work, so he decides to go into his garage that's full of mice and try to run them all over with his car.
